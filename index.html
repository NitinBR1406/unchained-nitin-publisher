About

What is Unchained Nitin Publisher?

Unchained Nitin Publisher is a private automation tool that enables content creator Nitin Ramdaras to publish short-form video content to TikTok and other social media platforms through an automated scheduling pipeline.

The application is built on top of Make.com and uses the TikTok Content Posting API to programmatically upload videos, manage captions, and schedule posts.

📅

Scheduled Publishing

Videos are automatically posted at pre-configured times for maximum reach.

🎬

Video Upload

Short-form video content is uploaded directly via the TikTok Content Posting API.

✏️

Caption Management

AI-generated captions and hashtags are applied automatically to every post.

🔒

Private Use Only

This app is used exclusively by its owner. No third-party accounts are accessed.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Unchained Nitin Publisher handles information when the application accesses the TikTok API. This is a private, single-user application owned and operated by Nitin Ramdaras.

2. Data We Access

The App requests the following TikTok API scopes:

  • video.publish — to upload and publish video content to TikTok on behalf of the owner.
  • user.info.basic — to verify the authenticated account identity.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal data from TikTok users. We do not access follower lists, DMs, comments, or any data beyond video publishing requirements. We do not sell or share data with third parties, and we use no analytics or tracking tools.

4. Data Storage

Video metadata (titles, captions, schedules) is stored in a private Google Sheets document accessible only to the owner. No TikTok user data is persisted by the App.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses Make.com as its automation backbone. Make.com privacy policy: make.com/en/privacy-notice.

6. Security

Access tokens are stored securely within Make.com encrypted credential vault and are never exposed in plain text or shared with third parties.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
