Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Unchained Nitin Publisher ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our TikTok content publishing application.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects and processes the following data to provide its publishing functionality:

  • TikTok account credentials — OAuth access tokens issued by TikTok to authorize video uploads on your behalf. These tokens are never stored in plain text and are only used during active publishing sessions.
  • Video content — video files and associated metadata (captions, hashtags, cover images) that you choose to publish through the App.
  • Publishing schedule data — dates, times, and platform settings you configure for scheduled posts.
  • Usage logs — automated logs of publishing actions (success/failure status, timestamps) for error handling and retry logic.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your TikTok account and publish videos on your behalf.
  • To schedule and automate content delivery according to your configured calendar.
  • To send you email notifications about published content and reshare reminders.
  • To log publishing results for troubleshooting and retry purposes.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. TikTok API Data

The App accesses TikTok's API under your authorization. We request only the minimum scopes required to publish video content. Data obtained through the TikTok API is used solely to perform the publishing actions you initiate and is not retained beyond what is necessary for those operations.

Your use of TikTok through this App is also subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage & Security

The App runs on Make.com's automation infrastructure. Data processed through automated workflows is handled in accordance with Make.com's privacy and security standards. We do not maintain our own servers or databases that store your personal information long-term.

Publishing metadata (post IDs, captions, schedule dates) is stored in a Google Sheets document accessible only to the App owner.

5. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are used during active sessions and are not stored permanently. Publishing records in Google Sheets are retained for operational purposes and can be deleted upon request. Email notifications are not stored by the App.

6. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Revoke the App's access to your TikTok account via TikTok's app settings.
  • Request deletion of any publishing records associated with your content.
  • Contact us with questions about how your data is handled.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
